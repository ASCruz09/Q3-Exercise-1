Tom Nook

Tom Nook is a raccoon who runs various businesses throughout the Animal Crossing series. He operates Nook's Homes, where the player can customize their house, and in Animal Crossing: New Horizons, he works at Resident Services and gives daily announcements prior to the building's upgrade.



Isabelle

Isabelle is a dog in the Animal Crossing series who first appeared in Animal Crossing: New Leaf, where she helps the player with their duties as mayor.


Mabel

Mabel Able is a hedgehog in the Animal Crossing series who co-owns the Able Sisters tailor shop with her older sister, Sable. She is the charismatic one out of the sisters, and she assists customers with their purchases.
